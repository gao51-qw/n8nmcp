Hi there 👋 — this is the first post on the **n8n-mcp** blog.

We'll use this space to share:

- Release notes and changelogs
- Deep-dives on the MCP protocol and how we wire it to n8n
- Performance + cost numbers from production traffic
- Tutorials for connecting Claude Desktop, Cursor and other AI clients

## Why a blog?

Most of our users are devs who want to know *how* something works before they
trust it with their automations. Short tweets and changelog entries don't
leave room for the kind of architectural detail we want to publish.

## What's next

Our next post will cover **how the knowledge base is built** — we scan
over 1900 n8n nodes (official + community) every week and ship them as a
single SQLite file inside the MCP container.

Stay tuned.
